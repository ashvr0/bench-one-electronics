02Privacy — what data this site collects

i

Short version: nothing leaves your browser. There's no account system, no server-side database, no analytics, and no tracking pixels anywhere in this guide.

Everything the guide remembers about your progress is stored using your browser's built-in localStorage — a small storage space tied to your specific browser, on your specific device. None of it is transmitted anywhere, and Anthropic, or whoever hosts this file, has no visibility into it.
